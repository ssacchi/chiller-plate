Asia Nunez

14 year old Digital Illustrator with 4 Visual Arts Scholarships. Started art at a young age, with classes at Spalding House, and Honolulu Museum of Art. Started two small businesses, as well as started commissions online. Collaborated with NOAA (National Oceanic Atmospheric Administration) with each business, donating a portion of proceeds to them for each sale. Talked to researchers and scientists that worked at NOAA, as well as observed and helped at a whaling museum for 5 years. First to have a dual STEAM based program and Art Certificate schedule at Mid-Pacific. Networked and met in person with many entrepreneurs from around the states. Was in Girls Who Code for 2 years. Created an Instagram with organic followers, grew an art style for almost 2 years now. Saw which content creators were making money, and how challenging it was for smaller creators to become bigger as well.

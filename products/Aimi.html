Sean Sakamoto

Illustrator, author, musician-songwriter, filmmaker, and student. Sole creator of a webcomic. Awardee of multiple state, national, individual, and collaborative achievements in academics, robotics, filmmaking, creative writing, and illustration. Directed, shot, edited, wrote, and (on one particular occasion) voiced documentaries for PBS Hawaiʻi’s HIKI NŌ program with the Kamehameha Schools Maui Middle School team from 2016 to 2018. Edited and shot the 2018 promotional for Maui Memorial Hospital’s Caregiver Week. Began co-tutoring Kamehameha Schools Maui Middle’s Math Counts team post-participation in the Math Counts, Math Matters, Maui Math Challenge, and Maui Math Circle programs and competitions throughout middle school. Networked with Maui and Hawai’i-based creators and business owners. Will begin creating animated films and wishes to begin experimenting with programming languages due to past experience in Sphero, Scratch, and LEGO Robotics block coding.

Company Bio

We are a team of students with a passion for the arts and a concern for the route our earth is heading. We have dedicated our time to a place where everyone is able to express themselves with equal opportunity all while saving the planet.

Clean, Friendly, Creative

Our interface incorporates designs of a tree that emphasizes our environmentally conscious purpose. We promote a safe and non-toxic space for people to come together from all over the world.

The health of the Earth is slated to reach the point of no return by the year 2030. Many people want to help, but have no idea how. This is where Rēcap comes in. Rēcap is a platform where content creators can share and monetize their works. With equal opportunities for small and large creators alike, everyone can contribute to the causes that they believe in. Through ads, users can choose to donate to charities aimed towards sustainability and environmental health or collect the revenue themselves. Viewers will be able to see what types of charities Rēcap creators donate to most often, and will know who to support to make a difference.
